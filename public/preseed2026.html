02 — Problem

Africa's builders can ship in a weekend.
Getting paid still takes weeks.

Every day a digital business cannot collect payments is a day of revenue lost.

Fragmented Rails
Every African market has different providers, different integrations, different failure points. Building across Africa means rebuilding your payment stack in every country.
Built for the Wrong Businesses
Paystack and Flutterwave were built for merchants and fintechs. Gaming platforms, software companies, creator platforms, digital marketplaces — nobody built for them.
Global Collection
To collect outside Africa with Stripe: register a US LLC, get an EIN, open a foreign bank account. Months of work before charging a single customer.
New Payment Types, No Infrastructure
Stablecoins are how tens of millions of Africans hold and move value. Credit billing and token metering are how AI products charge. No African PSP supports any of it.
